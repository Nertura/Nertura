## 1. Introduction

Nertura ("we," "us," "our") operates an AI-powered agriculture intelligence operating system available at nertura.com and app.nertura.com (the "Service"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect personal data when you use the Service.

We are committed to compliance with applicable data protection laws, including the **EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)** and the **Turkish Personal Data Protection Law (KVKK — Law No. 6698)**.

## 3. Personal Data We Collect

### 3.1 Account and identity data

Name, email address, phone number (optional), password hash (stored by our authentication provider), organization name, role, language preference, and timezone.

### 3.2 Farm and operational data

Farm names, field boundaries (which may include geolocation coordinates), crop plans, tasks, observations, harvest records, inventory records, and photographs you upload from fields. When linked to an identifiable individual or sole proprietorship, this data may constitute personal data under GDPR and KVKK.

### 3.3 AI interaction data

Questions you ask, photos submitted for disease detection, AI responses, confidence scores, feedback (confirm/reject/correct), and metadata about inference requests.

### 3.4 Payment data

Billing name, address, subscription tier, and transaction history. **Payment card details are processed directly by Stripe** and are not stored on our servers.

### 3.5 Communications data

Support messages, email engagement (opens/clicks if you consent to marketing), WhatsApp messages if you opt in to that channel.

### 3.6 Technical data

IP address, browser type, device identifiers, log files, cookies and similar technologies (see our Cookie Policy).

### 3.7 Consent records

Records of permissions you grant or withdraw, including timestamps, policy versions, and channel.

## 5. AI Processing and Automated Decision-Making

The Service uses artificial intelligence to provide recommendations, including crop disease identification from photographs. Outputs are probabilistic and intended as **decision support only**, not professional agronomic advice.

You may request human review of significant automated outputs by contacting privacy@nertura.com or using in-app feedback tools.

Training our global models on your data occurs **only if you opt in** separately from operational AI use. You may withdraw opt-in at any time without affecting core Service functionality tied to your subscription.

---

## 6. Photo Uploads and Location Data

When you upload field photographs, we process image content for disease detection and operational records. Unless you enable precise geotagging, we strip GPS metadata from images on upload.

By uploading photos, you confirm you have the right to share the image and that it does not unlawfully depict third parties.

## 10. Your Rights

### 10.1 GDPR (EEA/UK users)

You may have the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict processing, data portability, object to processing, and withdraw consent. You may lodge a complaint with your supervisory authority.

### 10.2 KVKK (Turkey users)

Under KVKK, you may have the right to learn whether data is processed, request information, learn the purpose, know third parties, request correction or deletion, object to automated analysis results against you where applicable, and claim compensation for unlawful processing.

**KVKK Application:** Submit requests to privacy@nertura.com with identity verification. We respond within **30 days** unless extended as permitted by law.

### 10.3 Exercising rights

Email privacy@nertura.com or use in-app Privacy settings (export/delete). We verify identity before fulfilling requests.
