## Quick Reference

**North Star:** Fields with at least one active case (`field_cases.status` in `open` or `monitoring`).

**The one monetization rule:** Charge for **memory, depth, and continuity** — never for the first moment of trust.

**The one marketing rule:** AI creates **drafts only**; humans approve before anything reaches a grower or prospect.

**The one brand rule:** Speak like a calm agronomist, not a Silicon Valley demo.
