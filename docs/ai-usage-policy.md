## 1. Scope

This AI Usage Policy applies to all artificial intelligence features in Nertura, including the AI Assistant, photo disease detection, proactive insights, and future AI capabilities.

---

## 2. What AI Does

Nertura AI analyzes your farm context (fields, crops, tasks, weather, history) and external models to provide suggestions, answers, and classifications. AI may use providers such as OpenAI, Google Gemini, and Anthropic Claude under Nertura's control and data agreements.

---

## 3. What AI Does Not Do

- Replace licensed agronomists or regulatory compliance obligations
- Guarantee correct disease identification in all cases
- Automatically apply chemicals, change irrigation, or execute financial transactions without your confirmation
- Provide medical advice for humans or animals

---

## 4. Your Responsibilities

- Verify AI outputs before field action, especially chemical applications
- Provide accurate context (crop type, field, growth stage)
- Report incorrect results using in-app feedback
- Not attempt to extract other users' data via prompt injection
- Comply with credit limits and fair use

---

## 5. Confidence and Human Review

Diagnosis results include confidence indicators. Low-confidence results may be queued for expert review where that feature is available. You may request human review via support.

---

## 6. Data Use for AI

**Operational use:** Your data is processed to serve your organization under our Terms and Privacy Policy.

**Global model training:** Occurs only with your **explicit opt-in**. Opt-in is separate from using AI features. Withdrawal stops future training use; does not delete past lawful processing logs.

We configure providers to minimize retention where commercially available.

---

## 7. Automated Actions

At launch, AI-suggested actions (e.g., creating tasks) require your **explicit confirmation** before execution.

Future autonomous features, if any, will require separate notice and controls.

---

## 8. Prohibited Uses

- Generating harmful, deceptive, or illegal content
- Harassment or impersonation
- Circumventing safety filters
- High-volume automated scraping of AI responses
- Submitting others' private field data without authorization

Violations may result in suspension.

---

## 9. Availability and Changes

AI features may change, be limited by tier, or be temporarily unavailable. We may update models and prompts to improve safety and accuracy.

---
