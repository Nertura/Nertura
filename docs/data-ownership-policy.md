# Nertura — Data Ownership Policy

> Who owns what data in the Nertura intelligence operating system — farmers, organizations, Nertura, and the global agricultural corpus — with rights, restrictions, and commercial use boundaries.

---

## Policy Statement

Nertura is built on a simple principle: **customers own their operational data; Nertura owns the platform and aggregated intelligence derived with consent.** We are stewards, not owners, of farmer and organization information. Trust is the foundation of a self-growing AI company.

This policy applies to all users, organizations, channels (web, mobile, WhatsApp, email, social), and AI-generated content on the platform.

**Effective:** Launch · **Jurisdiction:** Global with KVKK (Turkey) and GDPR (EU) alignment · **Legal companion:** `/docs/data-privacy-kvkk-gdpr.md`

---

## Definitions

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| **Customer Data** | Data uploaded or generated by users/orgs in using Nertura services |
| **Platform Data** | System logs, anonymized aggregates, consented global corpus |
| **AI Output** | Responses, diagnoses, media generated by Brain for a customer |
| **Derived Data** | Embeddings, memory nodes, graph edges computed from Customer Data |
| **Nertura IP** | Software, models (when trained), brand, curated corpora Nertura licenses |

## Customer Data — Detailed Rights

### You own

- All farm geometry, crop plans, harvest records, inventory, CRM contacts you create
- Photos and scouting observations you upload
- AI conversation history in your account
- Marketplace listings and orders where you are party
- White-label voice clones you commission (subject to voice agreement)
- Memory and graph nodes scoped to your organization

### Nertura may

- Store and process Customer Data to deliver subscribed services
- Create Derived Data (embeddings, summaries) to power your AI features
- Display your data back to authorized users in your organization
- Use **anonymized, aggregated** statistics that cannot identify you (e.g., "average corn yield region X") without consent
- Retain data as required by law (billing, audit)

### Nertura may NOT

- Sell identifiable Customer Data to third parties
- Share field-level data with sponsors without consent program opt-in
- Use Customer Data for global model training without `ai_training_global` consent
- Provide one customer's operational data to another customer
- Claim ownership of your harvest, trade secrets, or proprietary agronomic programs

## AI Output Ownership

| Scenario | Ownership |
|----------|-----------|
| AI diagnosis for your field | You own the outcome; Nertura stores copy for service continuity |
| AI-generated blog on your white-label site | You own published content; Nertura retains backup |
| Nertura brand TikTok script | Nertura IP |
| Crop plan draft from AI Agronomist | You; AI is advisory — you decide execution |
| Sponsored tip shown to you | Sponsor + Nertura; labeled disclosure |

**Advisory disclaimer:** AI outputs are recommendations, not professional agronomic, legal, or financial decisions. Liability for field actions remains with the user/organization.

## Consent-Gated Nertura Use

| Use | Consent required |
|-----|------------------|
| Operational AI (your RAG, your memory) | Terms + `ai_operational` |
| Global anonymized corpus | `ai_training_global` — **opt-in, default off** |
| Photo in global disease dataset | `ai_training_global` + photo checkbox |
| Sponsor-funded credits | `sponsor_programs` |
| WhatsApp AI | `whatsapp_ai` double opt-in |
| Marketing email | `marketing_email` |

Withdrawal stops **future** processing for that purpose; see deletion for past data.

## Data Portability

Organizations and users may export:

- Profile, farms, fields, crops, inventory, CRM
- AI interaction history (JSON)
- Photos (ZIP)
- Reports (PDF/CSV)
- Consent history

Export via Settings or privacy@nertura.com. Delivered within **30 days** (GDPR/KVKK).

---

## Data Deletion

| Request | Result |
|---------|--------|
| Delete account | User PII purged; org data handled per role |
| Delete organization | Full org cascade after confirmation |
| Delete specific photo | Removed from storage + de-indexed from RAG |
| Opt out training | No new global promotion; org copy retained |

Already-anonymized global nodes cannot be re-identified and are not deleted on individual request.

Details: `/docs/data-privacy-kvkk-gdpr.md`.

## Nertura Platform & Moat Data

Nertura retains ownership of:

|-------|-------------|
| **Anonymized global knowledge graph nodes** | Built from consented feedback |
| **Nertura Model weights** | Proprietary trained models |
| **Curated agronomic corpora** | Licensed reference material |
| **Platform analytics** | Product usage patterns (not sold raw) |
| **Brand content library** | Nertura marketing media |

This is the **compounding intelligence asset** described in `/ai/data-moat-strategy.md` — distinct from Customer Data.

## Termination

| Event | Customer Data | Derived / global |
|-------|---------------|------------------|
| Subscription ends | Read-only retention 12 months; then delete unless export |
| Account deletion | Purge per deletion workflow |
| Enterprise contract end | Negotiated data return + deletion certificate |
| Nertura insolvency | Customer Data returned or deleted per DPA; no sale as asset without consent [Enterprise DPA standard] |

---

## Subprocessors

Customer Data may be processed by vetted subprocessors (cloud, AI APIs, email, WhatsApp) under DPA. List published at `nertura.com/legal/subprocessors`. No subprocessor receives ownership.
