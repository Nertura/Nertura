## 1. What Are Cookies

Cookies and similar technologies (local storage, pixels) are small files stored on your device when you visit nertura.com or use app.nertura.com.

---

## 2. How We Use Cookies

| Category | Purpose | Legal basis (EU) |
|----------|---------|------------------|
| **Strictly necessary** | Authentication, security, load balancing, consent storage | Legitimate interest / necessary |
| **Functional** | Language preference, session state | Consent where required |
| **Analytics** | Understand usage (GA4, PostHog, Vercel Analytics) | **Consent** |
| **Marketing** | Attribution, campaign measurement | **Consent** |

We do not use advertising cookies at launch.

---

## 3. Cookies We Use

| Name / Provider | Type | Duration | Purpose |
|-----------------|------|----------|---------|
| Supabase auth cookies | Strictly necessary | Session / persistent | Login session |
| `nertura_consent` | Strictly necessary | 1 year | Cookie preference storage |
| `_ga`, `_ga_*` (Google) | Analytics | 2 years | GA4 — if consented |
| `ph_*` (PostHog) | Analytics | 1 year | Product analytics — if consented |
| Cloudflare `__cf_bm` | Strictly necessary | 30 min | Bot management |

List updated in cookie banner admin when tools change.

---

## 4. Cookie Banner

EU/EEA/UK/TR visitors see a banner with:

- **Accept all**
- **Reject non-essential**
- **Customize** (granular toggles)

Non-essential scripts load only after consent. Consent recorded in `consent_records`.

---

## 5. Managing Cookies

Browser settings can block cookies; blocking necessary cookies may break login.

Links: [Chrome](https://support.google.com/chrome/answer/95647), [Safari](https://support.apple.com/guide/safari/manage-cookies-sfri11471), [Firefox](https://support.mozilla.org/en-US/kb/cookies-information-websites-store-on-your-computer)

---

## 6. Do Not Track

We honor applicable opt-out signals where legally required (e.g., GPC where treated as opt-out).
